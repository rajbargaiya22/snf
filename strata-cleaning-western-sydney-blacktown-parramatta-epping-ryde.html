Strata Cleaning

SNF Cleaning is specialized in Strata Cleaning, Lawn Mowing and Gardening services. At SNF Cleaning we believe that our Cleaning and Gardening in common area creates positive environment , also maintains the value and age of your property. As every strata property is unique, with regards to its common property, history and the way in which the owner wish the property to be maintain, we tailor our service to suit the requirements of each owner corporation. We are committed to provide Quality services to our each of our Property.

We agree to a cleaning program that sets targets and standards. The frequency of cleans are determined by the client and also, we can help you to make it. We maintain our quality standard.

Our company is providing services to strata property over 4 years and we are growing company. We can work anywhere in Sydney.

Our service includes:-
  • Internal Cleaning
  • External Cleaning
  • Lawn Mowing, Edge trimming
  • Weed contro
  • Garden trimming
  • Bin In/Out and Washing

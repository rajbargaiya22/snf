ABOUT SNF CLEANING

SNF Cleaning is committed to provide trustworthy, honest and reliable professional services to its customers. We are working hard in the industry from last 8+ years. In this time we have gain trust of our clients. We have started from locally Parramatta, now serving Western Sydney, Canterbury area, Bankstown area and some of hills suburbs. Our team give excellent touch to work. We specialise in Strata services and also provide top-notch commercial cleaning services. Whether you are large or small facility you will get high quality and good customer service because we know that our professional cleaning creates your image for your place or facility.

We believe a simply neat and fresh environment is something that everyone deserves. So, simply we don't just tick a box, but fill a basic need by providing best service to your premises, we aim to build a long-lasting and rewarding relationship with all of our clients.

WHY CHOOSE US?

SNF Cleaning offers quality cleaning services and value for money. We work with our clients closely to determine their unique cleaning requirements, regardless of what industry they’re in, With highly trained staff. We go above and beyond to develop a relationship with our clients so that they feel completely comfortable with our service at all times. We’ll work with you to develop a cleaning solution that is perfectly tailored to your facility, business premises and we’ll proactively suggest any changes that could save you money, or have any other manner of positive impacts on your business.
